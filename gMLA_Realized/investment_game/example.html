Example

  • Suppose you invested 50 cents, and your success number was 4.
  • If your die roll is 4: your investment is successful. You can earn $3.00 (0.5*6) from your investment PLUS the 50 cents you did not invest (1-0.5), for a total bonus payment of $3.00 + $0.50 = $3.50.
  • If your die roll is any number other than 4: your investment is unsuccessful. You earn $0.00 from your investment PLUS the $0.50 ($1.00 - $0.50) you did not invest, for a total bonus payment of $0.00 + $0.50 = $0.50.
  • Note that investment amounts are restricted to 25-cent increments.
